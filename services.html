What Grooming Includes

Each pet will have its hair brushed, nails trimmed, ears cleaned and plucked, eye area cleaned, sanitary shave and the haircut of your choice.

All pets are washed twice in high quality shampoo and conditioner and hand fluff dried for optimal results.

Cologne and a bandana or bows are provided to complement your pet's hairstyle.

We specialize in custom styles, Bichons, and shed reduction.

Luxury Spa Services

For The Pampered Pet!

Fur Coloring

Pet safe, non-toxic fur coloring is a wonderful way to enhance a groom or celebrate a special occasion. Colors are temporary. Please ask for details and cost.

All-Natural Flea Baths

All-natural flea baths are provided for your pet's comfort at $10. The natural flea shampoo is designed to work with prescription flea preventatives from your vet.

Medicated and Hypoalergenic Shampoos

Medicated and hypoallergenic shampoos are available at your request for no additional charge. You are welcome to bring any shampoo prescribed by your vet.

Nail Painting and Nail Art

Nail painting and nail art are available for additional charges of $10-$20. Please ask the stylist about seasonal nail art and paint colors.

Special Needs Pets

Geriatric pets, disabled pets, and pets with medical conditions are welcome with written permission from your veterinarian.

At this time, services are not offered for aggressive pets or those that require sedatives for grooming.

All dogs must be less than 90 pounds in weight.
