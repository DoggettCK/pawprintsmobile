Frequently Asked Questions

Will my pet have to be up to date on vaccinations?

Yes, you will need to provide a current copy of your pet's rabies certificate.

Can you groom my cat too?

No, we are no longer accepting cats.

Can I see the mobile salon during my appointment?

Yes, you are encouraged to see the mobile salon before your pet is groomed but insurance prohibits visitors while grooming services are being performed.

How long does it take for my pet to be groomed?

Approximately 1 to 3 hours, depending on the type of pet and condition of the hair.

I have heard horror stories about pets dying in cage dryers, how will you dry my pet safely?

All pets are hand dried using only special pet dryers. This method is safer because your pet is not left unattended in a hot cage during the drying process.

Do you have to plug into my electric? What about water?

No. The Pet Spa operates on a state of the art inverter system that stores enough power to supply our needs all day long. We carry a 45 gallon fresh water tank and a 50 gallon grey water tank. Every pet is bathed in fresh water. WATER IS NOT RECYCLED BETWEEN PETS.

Will you bathe my pet in warm water?

Of course! Our mobile unit has a propane hot water heater that keeps our water warm from the first appointment to the last.
